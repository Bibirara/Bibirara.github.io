I am a assistant professor of economics at Illinois Wesleyan University. My research is in macroeconomics, international economics, applied time series (macroeconometrics), and environmental economics. The strength of my research lies in building and solving dynamic stochastic general equilibrium models to study business cycles and environmental issues in emerging market economies, and using structural vector autoregressions to conclude empirical evidence from data.
